--- IMPLEMENTED ---

Mountain map — no flat ground, only slopes:
  Added a dense grid of tilted terrain slab tiles (BoxGeometry 48x5x48, step 44 units)
  covering the entire mountain map. Each tile is rotated +/-18 degrees in both X and Z
  axes and elevated 0-3.5 m, so the player is always walking on a slope. Tiles are
  registered as slopeMeshes so getFloorHeight raycasts them correctly. The spawn area
  (+/-22 units) stays flat. Two rock materials alternate for visual variety. The existing
  240 large rock formations are preserved on top of the new terrain.

Quintuple zombies in Zombie Apocalypse mode:
  Wave 1 starting count: 90 -> 450  (main.js)
  Per-wave scaling formula: (70 + wave*45)*3 -> (70 + wave*45)*15  (entities.js)
  This is a true x5 multiplier on all zombie counts across every wave.

Other changes made in this session:
  - City map rebuilt as a modern city grid with roads, sidewalks, and buildings in rows.
  - Impact craters are now real traversable pits; crater walls extend above ground and a
    dark scorched ring marks each crater on the ground surface.
  - Performance optimisations: road dashes and rubble use InstancedMesh, building
    materials are shared objects instead of one-per-building allocations.
  - Reset cheat added: type "reset" in the cheat console (backtick key).
